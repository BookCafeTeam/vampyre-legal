Frequently asked questions

How do I create or join a room?
Open the app, tap Create Room or Join Room, and share the room code with friends. No account or sign-in is required.

How do I restore a purchase?
Go to Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchases.

How do I delete my data?
Email us at the address above and we will delete your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
