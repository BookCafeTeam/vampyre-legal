VAMPYRE — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vampyre ("the App", package name com.bookcafe.vampyre) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app. Vampyre is a multiplayer social deduction party game.

1. Information We Collect

  • Anonymous account identifier: a random ID created via Firebase Authentication (anonymous sign-in) to identify your device across sessions. No email, phone number, or real name is required.
  • Player profile: the display name and avatar image/illustration you choose in the app.
  • Gameplay data: match results, roles played, experience points, and statistics, stored to power your in-app profile and leaderboard-style stats.
  • Diagnostics: crash reports and performance data via Firebase Crashlytics, and usage analytics via Firebase Analytics (e.g. screens viewed, features used).
  • Advertising data: if you are not a Pro subscriber, Google AdMob (and its mediation partners, including Unity Ads) may collect device and advertising identifiers to serve and measure ads, subject to your consent choices where required by law (e.g. in the EEA/UK).
  • Subscription data: if you purchase Vampyre Pro, RevenueCat processes your subscription status and purchase events on our behalf. Payment details are handled entirely by Apple App Store / Google Play — we never see your card information.
  • Camera / Photo Library: if you choose to set a custom profile photo, the app requests access to your camera or photo library solely to let you pick or take that image.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate core gameplay (rooms, roles, voting, night actions) via Firebase Realtime Database.
  • To maintain your profile, stats, and progression across sessions.
  • To detect and fix crashes and improve app performance.
  • To show relevant ads (non-Pro users) and manage subscriptions (Pro users).
  • To respond to feedback you submit through the in-app feedback form.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers who help us run the app: Google Firebase (authentication, database, analytics, crashlytics, remote config), Google AdMob and Unity Ads (advertising), and RevenueCat (subscription management). These providers process data under their own privacy policies and only for the purposes described above. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Your Choices

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, stats, and stored game tokens.
  • Where required by law, you will be shown a consent prompt for personalized advertising and can withdraw consent at any time via your device's ad settings.
  • You can restore or cancel a subscription at any time via Settings → Subscription, which opens Apple/Google's native subscription management.

5. Children's Privacy

Vampyre is not directed at children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Retention

Gameplay and profile data is retained for as long as your account exists. Deleting your account removes this data from our active systems. Anonymized analytics data may be retained for longer for product improvement purposes.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to muhammet2000byt@gmail.com.
